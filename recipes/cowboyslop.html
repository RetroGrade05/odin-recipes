Description

Cowboy slop is a midwestern dish that is popular in camping for its ease of preperation, packable engredients, and high calorie and protein content. The easies way to describe its flavor is chile but with hot dogs, corn, and liquid smoke.

  • Corn
  • Hotdogs
  • ETC
